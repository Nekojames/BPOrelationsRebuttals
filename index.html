    20. ACCOUNT FROZEN
      Customer: "Why do I need to unlock my account for this soft credit pull?"
      I completely understand your hesitation. The reason we need to unlock your account is to allow us to perform the soft credit pull, which is completely free and non-invasive. This is a standard procedure that helps us move forward smoothly with the process. You have two quick options to unlock your account:
      1. Call the Credit Bureau – While this option may take a few days to process, it’s a reliable way to unlock your account.
      2. Use the Website for Instant Access – The fastest way is to unlock it through the bureau’s website, which only takes a moment and gives you immediate access.
      If you’re not comfortable calling, the website option is quick and secure. It’s just a few minutes of your time, and then we can proceed without any delays. We're here to make the process as smooth as possible for you.

      Customer has already unfrozen their account:
      If the customer informs you that the credit company has already unfrozen their account, modify or change the last digit of the SSN (e.g., if it ends in 1, change it to 2), then run the credit. If the credit is approved, revert the SSN to the correct number, SAVE, and then TRANSFER. If the credit is declined, follow the declined script.
