🐈NEKO'S CALL SCRIPT 🐈

Objections and Rebuttals

  • 1. "How did you get my information?"
    We work with secure, verified sources to identify people who might benefit from our financial solutions. Our goal is simple: connect you with the right options without the hassle. We take privacy seriously, so your information is always protected. (Pause briefly after 'simple' to let this sink in. Use a confident, reassuring tone. Rising intonation on 'simple' and falling intonation on 'protected'.)
  • 2. Hesitant to Provide SSN
    I completely understand. We only ask for your SSN to confirm your identity and match you with the best offers. It’s fully encrypted, secure, and never shared without your consent. We’re here to make this easy, not risky. (Slow down on 'fully encrypted' to emphasize security. Use a calm, trustworthy tone. Rising intonation on 'fully encrypted' and falling on 'without your consent'.)
  • 3. "What company is this?"
    We’re the Loan Assistance Center. We partner with a network of trusted lenders to help you find the right financial fit without the runaround. Our mission is to make your financial journey simpler, faster, and more transparent. (Pause after 'runaround' for impact. Use a professional but approachable tone. Slight rise on 'runaround' and confident fall on 'transparent'.)
  • 4. "Don’t do a soft pull—I have bad credit."
    Totally get it. But here’s the thing—a soft pull won’t affect your score. It’s a quick, risk-free way to see what you qualify for without the pressure. Why not see what options are on the table? (Pause after 'totally get it' to show empathy. Use a confident, reassuring tone. Rising intonation on 'risk-free' and falling on 'pressure'.)
  • 5. Wrong SSN or Info
    No problem. We just need accurate, verified information to make sure you’re matched with the right options. It’s all about keeping things smooth and hassle-free. (Use a supportive tone, like a friendly reminder. Rising slightly on 'accurate' and falling on 'hassle-free'.)
  • 6. "I’m not interested" / "Not looking for a loan."
    I hear you, and I respect that. But just to be clear, this isn’t just a loan offer. It’s about finding a financial solution that actually makes a difference. Would you be open to hearing a bit more about what that might look like? (Slow down slightly to show understanding. Use a warm, non-pushy tone. Rising intonation on 'difference' and sincere fall on 'look like'.)
  • 7. "What’s your exact location?"
    We’re based in Irvine, California, and we work exclusively with a national network of vetted lenders. Once you’re pre-qualified, you’ll get full transparency about the lender, terms, and next steps. Every piece of this process is built on trust, clarity, and compliance. (Provide information clearly with a calm tone. Rising intonation on 'trust' and falling intonation on 'compliance'.)
  • 8. "I don’t have time."
    I completely understand. That’s why we’ve made the process as quick as possible. Just a few minutes could change the course of your financial future. And this is a limited-time opportunity, so timing is important. (Slow down to make this sound more impactful. Use a slightly faster pace towards 'limited-time opportunity' to create urgency.)
  • 9. "Why unlock my account for a soft pull?"
    To proceed, your account needs to be unlocked so we can complete a soft pull, which doesn’t affect your credit score. It’s a quick, secure step to ensure we can offer the best terms possible for you. (Pause briefly after 'quick' to create emphasis. Rising intonation on 'best' and falling tone on 'possible'.)
  • 10. "What’s your website or address?"
    We’d be happy to provide that information. However, for your safety, we only send sensitive details through secure email. This ensures that everything you receive is accurate, verified, and sent securely. (Use a calm and professional tone. Rising on 'sensitive' and falling on 'securely'.)
  • 11. "Why don’t you have my information?"
    We work with multiple sources, but we always verify directly with you. This ensures we have accurate, tailored information to make sure you get the right offers without any confusion or missed opportunities. (Use a reassuring tone. Rising slightly on 'tailored' and falling on 'missed opportunities'.)
  • 12. "How are you different from other lenders?"
    We’re different because we put your financial well-being first. You’ll have access to low fixed rates starting at 5.99%, with no collateral required, and we approve based on your full financial profile—not just your credit score. (Confident tone. Rising intonation on 'access' and strong fall on 'profile'.)
  • 13. "Soft Pull vs. Hard Pull?"
    A soft pull allows us to check your pre-qualification status without affecting your credit score. It’s completely invisible to other lenders. A hard pull happens only when you’ve given full consent, so there’s no risk involved at this stage. (Use a calm tone, rising on 'risk' and falling on 'stage'.)
  • 14. "Why do you need my email?"
    Your email ensures that you get all the critical updates, documents, and approvals in real-time, securely. It keeps everything streamlined and helps us stay in touch without delays. (Slow down for clarity. Rising on 'updates' and falling on 'without delays'.)
  • 15. "I want info via email first."
    Absolutely, we respect your preference. To send the most accurate offer, we first need to confirm a few key details with you. Once that’s done, your personalized options will be delivered directly to your inbox—securely and right on time. (Use a friendly tone, rising on 'personalized' and falling on 'right on time'.)
  • 16. "I don’t have an email."
    That’s okay! But here’s the thing: missing this opportunity means missing out on options that could really benefit you. It’s quick, it’s easy, and our team is here to help—not pressure you. (Use a friendly, empathetic tone. Rising on 'missing out' and falling on 'not pressure you'.)
  • 17. Doesn’t Want to Talk to Loan Specialist
    I completely understand. But what you’re missing is not a sales pitch. It’s an opportunity to learn about financial solutions that could save you money and offer a better path forward. We’re here to help, not to push—just to guide you through your options. (Use a warm, reassuring tone. Rising on 'save you money' and falling on 'better path forward'.)
  • 18. When You Encounter a Customer Already Handled by a Closer
    I see here that you’ve spoken with our Senior Loan Advisor. They’ve been helping you with your loan options. Let me connect you with them directly to make sure we’re providing the best assistance. Please hold for a moment. (Use a calm and helpful tone, rising on 'best assistance' and falling on 'hold for a moment'.)
  • 19. Handled by Closer (Credit Pull Result: NO RECORD FOUND)
    If a credit pull returns 'NO RECORD FOUND,' it usually means the information provided doesn’t match. Let’s confirm your full legal name and billing address to ensure we have accurate details for the next step. (Use a calm, neutral tone. Rising slightly on 'accurate details' and falling on 'next step'.)
  • 20. If Account is Frozen, Locked, or Frozen
    I understand that unlocking your account may seem like a hassle, but it's quick and secure. Once it’s unlocked, we can continue smoothly with a risk-free soft pull. The credit bureaus can assist you with this process directly, and once unlocked, we can proceed immediately. (Use a supportive tone. Rising slightly on 'quick' and falling on 'proceed immediately'.)
