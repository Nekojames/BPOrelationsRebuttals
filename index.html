Objections and Rebuttals

    1. How did you get my information?

      We partner with trusted credit bureaus to match people with the best loan options. It’s a legal, secure process designed to protect your privacy while providing you with real opportunities to improve your financial situation.

    2. Hesitant to Provide SSN

      Your SSN is kept safe using encryption technology that meets the highest security standards. We only need it to verify your identity and provide you with the most relevant offers—no harm to your credit, just a secure way to get things done.

    3. What company is this?

      We’re Loan Assistance Center, working with reputable lenders nationwide to simplify your options. We’re not here to push anything on you—we’re here to make the process easier and connect you with trustworthy solutions.

    4. Don’t do a soft pull—I have bad credit.

      We don’t judge based on your credit score. Our lenders take a deeper look at your overall financial picture, and since it's a soft pull, your credit score won’t be impacted. It’s an opportunity to see where you stand, risk-free.

    5. Wrong SSN or Info

      We need accurate info to ensure we match you with the right lender and offer. If there’s an error in your details, it could delay the process, so let's fix it now to keep things moving smoothly.

    6. I’m not interested / Not looking for a loan

      That’s totally okay! But think of this as more than just a loan—this is about giving you more control over your finances. Whether it’s lowering debt or accessing better rates, this could be a chance for a fresh start, without any pressure to commit right away.

    7. What’s your exact location?

      We’re based in Irvine, California, and partner with a nationwide network of trusted lenders. Once you’re pre-qualified, we’ll share all the details about your lender, terms, and next steps with full transparency.

    8. I don’t have time.

      We understand! That’s why we’ve made the process as quick and simple as possible. Just a few minutes now could unlock a much better financial future, and we’re ready to help you every step of the way.

    9. Why unlock my account for a soft pull?

      Unlocking your account lets us do a soft inquiry, which has no effect on your credit score. It’s a quick and secure way for us to get the right info so we can offer you personalized loan terms that suit you best.

    10. What’s your website or address?

      We’ll happily share official info with you. For your protection, sensitive details will only be sent through secure email, ensuring everything is verified and safe from start to finish.

    11. Why don’t you have my information?

      We gather data from trusted sources, but we always double-check with you directly to make sure everything is accurate and tailored to you. It’s a way for us to avoid mistakes and ensure you get the best possible offer.

    12. How are you different from other lenders?

      Unlike traditional lenders, we look at the bigger picture and offer flexible, low rates starting at 5.99%. We’re focused on getting you the best possible terms, without the hassle, and we approve loans based on your overall financial story—not just your credit score.

    13. Soft Pull vs. Hard Pull?

      A soft pull lets us check your eligibility without affecting your credit score. Only after you’ve agreed to proceed will a hard pull take place. For now, we’re just trying to explore your options with zero risk to you.

    14. Why do you need my email?

      Your email is where we’ll send important updates, documents, and approvals. It’s the fastest, most secure way to keep you in the loop, without any delays or lost information.

    15. I want info via email first.

      Absolutely! First, we just need to confirm a few details to make sure we send you accurate information. Once that’s done, your personalized loan options will be sent to you securely via email, so you can review them at your convenience.

    16. I don’t have an email.

      No worries! We’ve helped plenty of people without email. It’s just a quick conversation, free of pressure, that could give you access to better loan terms and rates than you might expect.

    17. Doesn’t Want to Talk to Loan Specialist

      I totally understand—you’re not interested in a sales pitch. But a brief chat with a loan expert could help you get access to better rates, more flexible terms, and quicker approvals. No pressure, just a quick conversation to give you more options.

    18. Handled by Closer

      If you're already working with one of our Senior Loan Advisors, I’ll make sure they pick up where they left off. I see they were helping you with your application, and I’ll connect you right away so you’re not waiting any longer.

    19. No Record Found

      If no record was found, it likely means there was a small error in the info provided. Let’s just double-check your name and billing address to ensure everything matches up correctly so we can move forward.

    20. Why do I need to unlock my account for this soft credit pull?

      Unlocking your account is the only way for us to do a soft pull and ensure we have the right information to proceed. It’s easy and quick to do, and once it's unlocked, we can continue the process without any delays.

    21. When encountering a customer already being handled by a closer, don't just transfer them without context.

      It’s important to give the customer context before transferring. For example, you could say: "I see you spoke with our Senior Loan Advisor, and they were helping you with the program. I’ll connect you with them to pick up where they left off. Please hold on while I do that."
